## Overview
This project focuses on extracting Environmental and Social (ESG) data from publicly available sustainability and annual reports of global companies. The objective is to collect accurate, well-documented ESG metrics aligned with data quality and content analysis standards.

## Companies Covered
- Apple Inc.
- Unilever PLC

## Data Sources
- Apple Environmental Progress Report 2025 (FY2024)
- Unilever Sustainability Disclosures 2024

## Key ESG Metrics
- Greenhouse gas emissions
- Renewable energy usage
- Circular materials usage
- Social and supply-chain initiatives

## Methodology
- Reviewed official company sustainability reports
- Extracted ESG metrics with page-level references
- Flagged unavailable metrics as "Not Disclosed"
- Ensured data accuracy, consistency, and completeness

## Disclaimer
This project is for learning and portfolio purposes only. All information is sourced from publicly available disclosures.
